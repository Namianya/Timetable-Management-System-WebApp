COMPUTER SCIENCE EVENTS AFFECTING LECTURES

IBM TRAINING EVENT
IBM research lab will be carrying a training on 2/5/2014. The training will be from 8:00am to 5:00pm. All computer science students are allowed to attend the training but it is of more value to fourth year and third year students. Those willling to present are required to register in the faculty secretarial pool. Students and leccturers who will present will be awarded certificates
